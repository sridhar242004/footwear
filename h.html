Our Heritage

Since 1947, MAISON STEPS has been crafting exceptional footwear, preserving traditional techniques while embracing innovation.

1947

Founded by master craftsman Pierre Steps in Paris, establishing the foundation of luxury shoemaking.

1960

Expansion into international markets and introduction of our signature craftsmanship techniques.

1985

Launch of our bespoke service, setting new standards in personalized luxury footwear.

Present

Continuing our legacy of excellence while embracing sustainable practices and innovative techniques.
